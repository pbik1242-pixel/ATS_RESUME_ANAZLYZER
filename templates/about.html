How it works

A guided flow from upload to improvement

The process is intentionally simple: paste the job description, upload the resume, and then review the score, missing terms, and revision guidance.

01 Input

Upload

Add the job description

Paste the role details so the analyzer can look for skills, responsibilities, and wording that the ATS will care about.

02 Analysis

Analyze

Upload your resume

Compare your resume against the job description and surface the ATS score, matched terms, missing skills, and readability clues.

03 Iteration

Improve

Review and improve

Use the feedback to make focused edits, then re-run the analysis to watch the score and coverage improve.
