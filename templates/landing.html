What this website is about

Clear feedback for resume-to-job fit

ATS Resume Analyzer helps job seekers understand how well a resume matches a job description, which keywords are missing, how readable the resume is for screening, and what to improve before applying again.

What users see

Upload a resume, paste a job description, and get a practical breakdown instead of a vague pass or fail. The page shows the ATS score, keyword match, missing terms, readability signals, and the revision path that matters next.

Clarity first Everything is explained in plain language so the next step is obvious.
Proof in the score Users can compare progress across revisions instead of guessing what improved.
Guided improvement The feedback points to the missing keywords, skills, and readability issues first.

Outcome A faster, more focused resume review process that helps users move from uncertainty to action.
