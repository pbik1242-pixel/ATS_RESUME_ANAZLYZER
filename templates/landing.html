What this website is about

One guided path from upload to export

ATS Resume Analyzer helps job seekers understand how well a resume matches a job description, what is missing, and how to turn that feedback into a cleaner final version.

What users see

Upload a resume, paste a job description, and get a practical breakdown instead of a vague pass or fail. The page shows the ATS score, keyword match, missing terms, readability signals, and the next fix to make.

Clarity first Everything is explained in plain language so the next step is obvious.
Proof in the score Users can compare progress across revisions instead of guessing what improved.
Guided improvement The feedback points to the missing keywords, skills, and readability issues first.
Outputs ATS score, keyword coverage, missing skills, readability signals, and a revision path you can use immediately.
Outcome A faster, more focused resume review process that helps users move from uncertainty to action.
Tone Professional, practical, and centered on clear improvements instead of generic marketing language.
